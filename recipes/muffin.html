Blueberry Muffins

Description

Moist and delicious blueberry muffins.

Ingredients

  • Betty Crocker Wild Blueberry muffin and quickbread mix
  • 2/3 cups water
  • 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat oven as directed on box
  2. Place paper baking cups in muffin cups, or grease bottoms of each muffin cup
  3. Drain blueberries, rinse and set aside
  4. Stir muffin mis, water, oil and eggs in medium bowl just until blended )batter may be lumpy(
  5. Gently stir in blueberries and divide batter among muffin cups
  6. Bake as directed on box or until golden brown and tops spring back when touched
  7. Cool 5 minutes, loosen and carefully remove from pan
