Air Fryer Potatoes

Description

Golden crispy potatoes prepared in the air fryer. This recipe is incredibly easy to make and perfect to add to any breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning of choice

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into three quarter inch cubes
  2. Toss cubed potatoes in olive oil
  3. Cook potatoes in air fryer for 15 minutes on 400 degrees, tossing potatoes every 5 minutes
  4. Once finished cooking, toss potatoes in small amount of olive oil and top with your seasoning of choice
  5. Enjoy your perfectly air fryed potatoes
